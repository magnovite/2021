  • STEP-UP - Shoe Design Event


    This event aims to make the participants research on materials, as well as ergonomic requirements, to develop an innovative shoe design that fits the topic given.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. Participants can participate individually or in teams of 2.
    2. Submission requirements : sketches and a prototype (Digital renders or handmade).
    3. A short description of 75 words or less about the design is mandatory.
    4. PROCESS : Participants must make sketches of the shoe design and a prototype to show the form of the designed shoe based on the theme provided.
    5. SUBMISSION FORMAT : Submission (PDF) titled STEP UP_TEAM NUMBER (example, team number 1 should submit as STEP UP_TEAM 1)

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1.Follow the design brief from the event website.
    2.Team size - 1-3 members.
    3.All text must be in English.

    6.20 entries will be shortlisted for the jury presentation which will be held on 27th March 2021, on an online platform.

  • REFRAME-INTO THE PAST - Illustrative Read Aloud Event

    Participants are provided with an interesting opportunity to consider a particular event in history and put forward a storyboard showcasing their ideas

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1. 2 members in a group.
    2.Participants select a historical topic from the given options and create illustrations on how they would reimagine it to be, by altering the actual story.
    3.The illustrations can be shown as a set of storyboards or animation.
    4.Participants must present these illustrations along with any form of script (poem/speech) which will be read out during the presentation of the illustrations.
    5.The illustrations can be hand drawn or digitally made.
    6.The illustrations must not contain any form of text other than dialogues.
    7.A total of 5 minutes will be given for the presentation of the illustrations along with the narration.
    8.Offensive or sensitive content will not be qualified for the performance.
    9.Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
