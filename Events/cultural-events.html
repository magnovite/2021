  • INDIAN MUSIC BAND

    Leave your mark and vocalize your truth. Make your instruments the extension of your body.

    Description and Rules

    General Rules:
    1.Participants can select any Indian genre of music.
    2.No more than 4 teams are allowed per college. The Band should consist of a minimum of 3 and a maximum of 8 members per team.
    3.Time limit: 4-8 minutes. A maximum of 2 songs is allowed within the time frame. Exceeding the time limit will lead to negative marking.
    4.Songs can be sung in any Indian language.
    5.Drums recording or drum pad is allowed whereas drums programming or sequencing is not permitted.
    6.Raw mix is allowed. Any sophisticated editing or special effects will lead to disqualification of the team.
    7.Bands will be disqualified for misconduct, obscenity, or profanity used. The dress code should be decent and presentable in your video.

    For more details refer to the rulebook and brochure in homepage.
